About Us

GAS Logistics Plus® was established in 2013 to serve the growing business needs of Big transportation providers in the Sierra Leone. The purpose of our company is to provide the highest quality logistics services for Sierra Leone carriers and trading companies. Currently we are managing more than 350 trucks of our customers’ fleets, and together we are growing every year. After just three years in business we have expanded our operation to more than 30 people and succeeded in building a strong team of devoted professionals, providing the best service in the industry. Since 2014 the company has been trading in food products aimed at the Africa market. We are currently exporting a variety of African foods to our countrymates across West Africa.

Company and Transport

GAS Logistics Plus® is a 21st Century Logistics Company™ and a leading worldwide provider of transportation, warehousing, fulfillment, global logistics, business intelligence, technology, and supply chain solutions. The company was founded over 9 years ago in Freetown, Sierra Leone. Today GAS Logistics Plus® has annual global sales of over $400M and more than 1,000 employees located in 30+ countries worldwide. GAS Logistics Plus® is recognized as one of the fastest-growing privately-owned transportation and logistics companies, a top 3PL, a top freight brokerage and warehousing provider, a leading project cargo manager, and a great place to work. With our trademark Passion for Excellence™, we put the plus in logistics by doing the big things properly, plus the countless little things that ensure complete customer satisfaction and success. We manage Global Logistics + So Much More!
